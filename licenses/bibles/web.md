# World English Bible (`web`)

## Source Files Reviewed

- `English/web/eng-web.usfx.xml`

## Rights Summary

- The preface inside the USFX explicitly states that the World English Bible is
  in the public domain.
- It says it may be freely copied, distributed, redistributed, incorporated
  into software, and sold, with the expectation that the name "World English
  Bible" is reserved for faithful copies.

## Repository Decision

- `rightsStatus`: `public_domain`
- `allowDownload`: `true`
- `allowBundling`: `true`

## Notes

This is the strongest rights statement among the current sources and is a good
model for future production entries.
